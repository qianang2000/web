About Us

Welcome to Badger Mart, where quality meets convenience. Founded in 1995, Badger Mart began as a small, family-owned store with a mission to provide our community with fresh, locally-sourced produce and high-quality groceries. Over the past two decades, we've grown into a beloved neighborhood staple, known for our exceptional customer service and dedication to sustainability.

Our Mission

At Badge Mart, our mission is to offer a diverse selection of the freshest and healthiest food options while supporting local farmers and producers. We are committed to: Providing a wide range of organic and non-GMO products, ensuring our shelves are stocked with locally-sourced and seasonal produce. and offering fair prices and maintaining transparent relationships with our suppliers.

Our Values

We believe in offering the best. Our team carefully selects every product on our shelves to ensure it meets our high standards of quality and freshness. We are more than just a grocery store; we are a part of your community. We support local events, sponsor neighborhood initiatives, and provide a gathering place for friends and families. From sourcing to packaging, we prioritize environmentally-friendly practices. We aim to reduce our carbon footprint and promote sustainability through our operations and product choices.

Our Team

Our dedicated team is the heart of FreshHarvest. From our knowledgeable store managers to our friendly cashiers, each team member is committed to making your shopping experience enjoyable and hassle-free. We believe in continuous training and development to ensure our staff can provide expert advice and personalized service.
